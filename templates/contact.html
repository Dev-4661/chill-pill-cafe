Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is parking available?

Yes, we have valet parking available for our guests. Street parking is also available in the vicinity.

Is the restaurant wheelchair accessible?

Absolutely. We have wheelchair-accessible entrances, seating, and restrooms to ensure comfort for all guests.

Can you accommodate large groups?

Yes! For groups of 10 or more, please contact us directly so we can arrange special seating and customize your experience.

Do you offer vegetarian/vegan options?

We have extensive vegetarian options and can customize many dishes for vegan guests. Our menu clearly marks all dietary information.

Is Chill Pill Café family-friendly?

Yes, families are very welcome! We provide high chairs and have a kids' menu available upon request.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and digital wallets (Paytm, Google Pay, PhonePe).

Do you have Wi-Fi?

Yes, complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available for all our guests. Ask your server for the password.

Is smoking allowed?

Chill Pill Café is a non-smoking establishment to ensure a pleasant environment for all guests.
